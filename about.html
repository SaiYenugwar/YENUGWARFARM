ABOUT US

At Yenugwar Farm, we specialize in rice farming, mango farming, and fish pond management. Our farm boasts a wide variety of fish species, as well as delicious and nutritious rice and mango crops. We take great pride in our commitment to sustainable agriculture practices and strive to provide our customers with the highest quality products possible. Come visit our farm and experience the beauty and bounty of Yenugwar Farm!
